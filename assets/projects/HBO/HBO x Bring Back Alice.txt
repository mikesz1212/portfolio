HBO x Bring Back Alice 
Production 

Client: HBO 
Bring Back Alice is a Polish HBO Max original series - a dark mystery/thriller set in the TriCity.The campaign required high-impact audiovisual and key art promo materials tailored for streaming, broadcast, and social platforms.
I worked as a Production Manager for the promotional assets, overseeing end-to-end execution: schedules and budgets, crew and vendor coordination, locations and permits, onset operations, and postproduction workflows through final delivery (trailer, key visuals, social cutdowns).
