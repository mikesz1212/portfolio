IGA ŚWIĄTEK X LANCÔME - GLOBAL BRAND PARTNERSHIP
production & creative

Client Lancôme

Iga Świątek and Lancôme announced a partnership that sees the world’s top tennis player become the first Polish woman to join the brand’s global ambassador roster.

I produced the ELLE cover stories created as part of the collaboration and delivered all campaign photo and video assets end-to-end, alongside strategic and creative advisory to align Lancôme’s luxury positioning with Iga Świątek’s brand.
